SDN Spanish

Learn Spanish Easily!

SDN Spanish is an interactive app that helps you learn Spanish from scratch. Improve your vocabulary, practice grammar, and complete fun exercises!

Features:

  • Structured lessons for beginners and advanced learners
  • Interactive exercises with instant feedback
  • Audio pronunciation by native speakers
  • Offline mode to study anytime, anywhere
  • Daily practice reminders

Built with: Kotlin, Firebase, Room Database, and Flutter
